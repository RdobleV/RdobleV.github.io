Topics

Welcome to my website with product design, electronics, robotics, photography, 3D-printing and other various projects. It is a mix of educational, profesional and hobby projects. Use the filters on the top to narrow down

Magnetic pen

A pen designed to handle small steel objects for assembly purposes.

3D-printed pen holder

A different nozzle on a 3D-printer allows for different designs and types of print. This pen holder is printed with a large nozzle to keep print time limited.

Powerful lamp

This lamp is built around a 20 W power LED to provide a room with a lot of light. Placed near a wall, the lamp emits its light and it reflects off the wall and ceiling.

Direct air capture

My graduation project in which a design for a device for capturing CO2 and water from air was made. These chemical compounds are used in a methanol plant.

Smart wall socket

This socket does not only provide power, but switches devices, saves power and responds to the user. An adaptable design allows for user customization.

Game progress tracker

A cube that helps you to keep your gaming sessions within a healthy limit, while providing progress tracking and an interesting object at then and of they day.

VR controller

A modular VR controller to be used with Manus VR gloves. A controller with shape changing features for higher immersion and supporting multiple game styles.

Unilever brand revival

An assignment where a new design was created to revive a declining brand. A novel product proposal was presented. This project is under a strict NDA.

Responsive lamps

Exploring the possibilities of IoT systems, a network of two smart lamps, a microcontroller and a server was created, to make data responsive light bulbs.

Light safety seat

Carrying a young child can be fairly heavy and exhausting. Regular safety seats are made of heavy materials. A carbon fiber seat however, is much lighter.

Connected LED matrix

A programmable LED matrix array built around the ESP8266. Connected to the internet, this device can display all kinds of data. A physical chart.

Printed phone holder

This 3D-printed phone holder enables the cyclist to use their phone as bike computer while not having to worry about it falling on the road.

Micro bike

A small model of a bike using nurbs modeling for multi-material 3D printing. A combination of flexible and rigid materials is used to make this bike in a single print.

Sustainable solar light

A project which analyses a WakaWaka product and improves on design and business model in terms of sustainability and circular economy.

IoT weather station

A solar powered weather station, monitoring temperature, humidity and light intensity. Connected via MQTT, the recorded data is sent on a local server.

Wake-up light

An Arduino-based wake-up light. A power LED, a realtime clock, a few buttons and a couple other components make a reliable and comfortable wake-up every day.

Simple storage rack

What do you do when you need some more storage space and a place for a 3D printer? A rack for 8 plastic boxes, storage underneath and a nice surface area on top.

VR monocle

A small handheld VR experience for one eye. A small two person project where an unusual use of VR technology was applied, to make a futuristic personal assistant.

LED array

Turning a bunch of used LEDs with damaged pins into a bright light. A prototyping board, switch, battery, resistors and some left over material make an effective build.

Balancing robot

This mechatronics project involves a sphere that can move around with a lot of control through a carefully chosen combination of software and hardware.

Flash light mount

A 3D-printed clip to use a Cree Q5 on a bike. This AA powered lamp provides more than enough light to safely ride around in the dark. Click on and go.

Cheese grater

Grating cheese in a new way, but already known to man for almost a hundred years. In this project a patent was turned into a modern product.

Stackable storage boxes

To find the best use of storage space, a modular storage box system was developed. It can fit in every room and be customized to the user needs.

Pasta maker

A design for a pasta maker which makes that you can extrude your own pasta right at your kitchen counter. Different caps allow you to make pasta of many shapes.

Thermal paste replacement

When your laptop runs hot, it may be time to replace the thermal thermal paste if it is degraded. Cleaning up both CPU and GPU is not hard after all.

Thin phone concept

A phone concept design that provides a big screen and a slim body compared to the devices available at the time. A design with an unique look and feel.
